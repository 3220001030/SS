1. Now 港闻 -  (三跑环评司法覆核败诉　机管局欢迎裁决) 2016-12-22    

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

【Now新闻台】3名市民入禀司法覆核环保署向机场第三跑道工程批出环评报告及环境许可证，高等法院裁定三人败诉。法官认同环评指，三跑对生态的影响只是暂时性。入禀司法覆核的3名市民包括保育人士何来、土地正义联盟成员谭棨禧，以及环保触觉成员余显璧，指环保署署长就机场第三跑道的工程批出环境许可证时，未有充份评估兴建三跑对生态及空域影响。高等法院法官周家明颁下书面判词，驳回他们的申请。

申请人认为政府假设了珠三角的机场已经达成开放空域协议，而这个假设应包含在三跑的环评报告内。但法官认为，环评报告毋须考虑空域争议，只需要根据机管局提供的现行运作推算数据，包括航班升降数字、跑道容量及机种等已经足够评估新跑道对噪音及空气污染的影响，认为环评报告并无不足。

对于入禀人认为，环评未有考虑三跑填海工程对中华白海豚等生态影响。法官就认为，环保署署长曾经考虑在工程期间兴建海岸公园，但后来发现并不可行，所以将措施延至2023年三跑运作后才进行。法官认同环评报告指生态影响属于暂时性，将来工程完结后是可逆转的。

法官最终裁决申请人败诉，3人并需支付讼费。

机管局欢迎裁决，又指三跑工程已启动，会继续按环境许可证的要求，落实对环境影响的缓解措施。

The general manager of the Airport Authority's Third Runway Division, Daman Lee, said on Tuesday it would not be possible to provide a new habitat for Chinese White Dolphins before the construction of the airport’s proposed new runway.

The project has a number of mitigation measures, including the establishment of a marine park after the completion of the runway, which is scheduled to take seven years.

Concern groups say the habitat loss during this period could lead to the dolphin population in Hong Kong being completely wiped out. They say they believe a new habitat should be provided for the dolphins before the project starts, to provide the dolphins with a sanctuary where they can continue to forage.

They are currently trying to halt work through a judicial review of the project’s Environmental Impact Assessment.

But Lee said Hong Kong is not the only natural habitat for these dolphins. He said that he believes the dolphins will go to their other natural habitats in the Pearl River Delta during the construction work.

“During the construction phase, we try to encourage some studies in terms of good conservation strategy,” he said. “Towards the end of the construction, we want to establish a marine park which is good for the long-term conservation of dolphins in this area.”

Lee added that they are taking a long-term conservation approach, and they will try to whatever they can to make sure that the waters are suitable for the Chinese White Dolphin.
